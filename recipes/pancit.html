Pancit

Description

Pancit, a beloved Filipino noodle dish, comes in many varieties, each with its unique combination of ingredients and flavors. A common version uses a mix of meats and vegetables, all stir-fried together to create a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. This recipe typically includes a combination of chicken, pork, and sometimes shrimp, which are sautéed with garlic, onions, and a colorful array of vegetables such as carrots, cabbage, and snow peas. The dish is seasoned with soy sauce and fish sauce, giving it a savory depth that complements the natural sweetness of the vegetables.

The noodles used in pancit are usually rice noodles or egg noodles, which absorb the flavors of the stir-fry beautifully. The dish is often garnished with chopped green onions and served with wedges of calamansi or lemon for a fresh, citrusy kick. Pancit is not only a delicious and satisfying meal but also a symbol of long life and prosperity in Filipino culture, often served at celebrations and special occasions. Its versatility and flavorful profile make it a favorite among Filipinos and a delightful introduction to the rich culinary traditions of the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces rice noodles or egg noodles
  • 1/2 pound chicken breast or thigh, sliced into thin strips
  • 1/2 pound pork, sliced into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup shrimp, peeled and deveined (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 small cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup snow peas, trimmed
  • 1/2 cup sliced green beans
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon or calamansi wedges (for serving)
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Noodles

    • If using rice noodles, soak them in warm water for about 10 minutes, or until they soften. If using egg noodles, cook them according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook the Meat

    • In a large pan or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
    • Add the sliced chicken and pork, cooking until browned and cooked through. If using shrimp, add them and cook until pink. Remove the meat from the pan and set aside.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics

    • In the same pan, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil.
    • Sauté the sliced onion and minced garlic until fragrant and the onion is translucent.

  4. Cook the Vegetables

    • Add the carrots, cabbage, snow peas, and green beans to the pan. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.

  5. Combine and Season

    • Return the cooked meat to the pan with the vegetables.
    • Pour in the chicken broth, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Stir to combine.
    • Add the noodles, gently tossing to coat them with the sauce and distribute the ingredients evenly.
    • Cook for about 5-7 minutes, until the noodles have absorbed the liquid and are fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve

    • Transfer the pancit to a serving platter.
    • Garnish with chopped green onions and serve with lemon or calamansi wedges on the side.
